Hello! My name is Jennifer and I am rising junior at Yale University studying computer science. I am a proud member of Trumbull College at Yale. On campus, I am involved with the Yale Undergraduate Prison Project as GED tutor for justice-impacted individuals.

I am originally from San Bernardino, California, which is where I am currently located. I love spending time outdoors, whether just in my own yard, a nearby park, or some other nearby attraction. Being located in the Inland Empire means I have mountains, the beach, and the desert all within my reach, which I am very grateful for!

In my free time, I like to go on walks, do yoga, rewatch my favorite shows, and listen to music. I also like to paint (I'm bad at it) and play guitar, though I don't do it as much as I would like to. During quarantine I got into skateboarding a little bit and I would like to continue during the summer. :)
